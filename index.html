About The Owner

Growing up around animals, Ashley naturally grew an intense love for the companionship they provide us and understands just how much your pet means to you. They are part of the family. She grew up looking after foster kittens, her parents owned a pet store growing up, and now they own a hobby farm where she has had the pleasure of taking care of goats, pigs, chickens, donkeys, rabbits, and much more. Living in Toronto, she misses being around all of the animals and frequently looks after her friend’s pets while they are away. Therefore, turning this into a professional service to offer others just seemed to make sense.

Home Visits

Going away on vacation? Working late?

Whatever the case may be we’ll make sure your pet is fed and has everything they need while you are away. We will also provide you with updates and pictures!

Going away and want someone to look after your plants? Contact us with your plants needs and we are happy to take care of them. We also offer basic cleaning and gardening services for your balcony or yard.

Saltwater, reefs or freshwater.

We have experience in both freshwater and saltwater aquariums for those tank owners looking for advice, cleaning (vacation maintenance), feeding, and provide check-ins while you are away.
